About Us

WonderBees Services

At WonderBees Services, childcare isn’t just our business—it’s our family heritage. We’re devoted to pairing families with impeccable caregivers, taking pride in each perfect match. With an encouragement to upskill as certified Newborn Care Specialists, our nannies offer unparalleled care. Embark with us on a heartfelt voyage, where we elevate lives and bolster families, one magical match at a time!

Our story

How we care, day to day

Our care specialists are thoroughly screened, CPR certified, and selected for both professional skill and emotional intelligence. We support families that need reliable care at home, in hotels, and during postpartum transitions—always with the same depth of vetting and heart you expect from a heritage-led team.

Whether you are hiring for the long term or booking coverage for a single evening, we listen first, then match you with someone who complements your child’s routine and your family’s values.
