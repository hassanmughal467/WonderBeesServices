Which package fits your family?

Signature and Premier both include the full placement path you expect from a boutique agency: discovery, matching, interviews, vetting, offer support, and help navigating employment paperwork and payroll introductions. The experience and team attention are the same; the distinction is how much support you want after the nanny starts.

Signature pairs well with families who want a thorough search, confident onboarding, and a defined replacement guarantee window—without anticipating heavy advisory needs past the first season.

Premier adds a longer guarantee period and more runway for post-placement questions, schedule adjustments, and backup coverage planning—often chosen for first-time household hires, complex schedules, or when multiple caregivers are in the mix.

You do not have to commit to a tier on day one. Many families choose the package that matches their comfort level at the time of hire; we are happy to talk it through on a short call.
