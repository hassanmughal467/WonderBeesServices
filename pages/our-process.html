Our Process

How we match families and care specialists

We follow the same arc families expect from a dedicated placement team: deep discovery first, careful introductions next, and steady support after someone starts. Below is how each chapter unfolds when you work with WonderBees Services.

Creating the bond

  • We open with a real conversation—by phone, in our office, or in your home—then guide you through a structured intake that explores schedules, values, discipline style, travel, pets, and the nuances that make your household unique. When you are ready for us to begin the active search, a nonrefundable retainer applies (your agreement outlines the exact amount and how it credits toward placement).
  • From those conversations we draft a family profile that becomes the north star for every introduction—priorities at the top, negotiables clearly marked, so candidates know what success looks like from day one.
  • During screening we coach you on the traits and competencies that tend to predict great long-term fits, drawing on what has worked for families with similar rhythms. Caregivers move forward only after references, work history, and interviews meet our standard for “ready to meet your family.”

Presenting exceptional possibilities

  • We take matching seriously: when helpful, we layer in additional assessments or structured prompts so you can compare candidates on temperament, communication style, and approach to boundaries—not résumés alone.
  • When you are ready, we coordinate interviews (and trial time when appropriate) so you can observe chemistry in real life—not only on paper.
  • After each conversation we gather your feedback through a simple scorecard so impressions stay organized and everyone remembers what mattered most in the moment.

Discovering your perfect addition

  • Once you identify a front-runner, we help you interpret signals, check references again if needed, and align on realistic start dates and onboarding steps.
  • We support transparent compensation conversations—base pay, guaranteed hours, benefits, mileage, and contract guardrails—so offers feel fair to everyone involved.
  • Before anyone begins, we produce a thorough work agreement that reflects what you discussed, so expectations are explicit and both sides sign with confidence.

Assessing the growth of the relationship

  • After placement, we check in on a rhythm that matches your family—short calls early on, then periodic touchpoints—to make sure the partnership feels sustainable.
  • Depending on the service tier you select, a replacement window may apply if the fit proves wrong through no fault of your own. Your written agreement spells out timelines and conditions in plain language.
  • We treat every placement as a long-term relationship, not a transaction. If questions arise months later—about schedules, bonuses, or renegotiating duties—you can still reach us for guidance.
